Simple checklists you can copy into a notebook, spreadsheet, or budgeting app.

Weekly Review

25-minute review checklist

  • Check actual checking balance.
  • Subtract bills due before next payday.
  • Scan recent transactions for drift.
  • Pick one adjustment for next week.
  • Write the next payday plan.

Cash Buffer

Checking buffer setup

  • Choose a starter floor such as $100, $250, or $500.
  • Move small leftovers into the buffer.
  • Do not count the buffer as spending money.
  • Review the floor after two months.

Debt

Debt payoff pause rule

  • Keep minimum payments current.
  • Pause extra payments only for a named reason.
  • Set a restart condition in writing.
  • Keep tracking balances during the pause.
