# AdSense Site Restructure Design

Date: 2026-06-22

## Goal

Refresh MoneyMind into a focused personal-finance publication that looks actively maintained, credible, and useful enough for AdSense review.

## Direction

Keep the static HTML site, existing domain, and MoneyMind identity. Rework public entry pages around a small independent author voice, practical reader paths, and curated evergreen content. Add recent article updates that sound specific and lived-in rather than generic.

## Scope

- Do not modify `ads.txt`.
- Rebuild `index.html` and `blog.html` around curated topic paths.
- Refresh trust pages that explain author identity, corrections, and reader contact.
- Add recent article pages with practical examples and internal links.
- Update `sitemap.xml` for the public pages and new articles.
- Remove public emphasis from older hype-like or thin entries without deleting source files.

## Public Positioning

MoneyMind is a small personal-finance site by Jake Miller. It focuses on budgeting, debt payoff, emergency funds, household planning, and beginner investing. The site should avoid inflated claims and make clear that content is educational, not financial advice.
