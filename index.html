How This Site Is Maintained

MoneyMind is intentionally small. I would rather keep a smaller set of articles useful than publish a new generic list every day. Most updates come from ordinary money friction: a bill I forgot to plan for, a reader question, or an example that needs clearer numbers. When a reader points out an unclear example, a broken link, or a number that needs a second look, I review the page and update it when the change helps.

The articles are educational and based on ordinary personal finance experience. They are not financial, tax, legal, or investment advice for your specific situation. For personal decisions with serious consequences, work with a qualified professional.
